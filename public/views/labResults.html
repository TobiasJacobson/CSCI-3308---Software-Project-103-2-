Your Lab Results

Your lab results are very important to us here at NuraHealth. We promise to make sure that all of your lab results are delivered to you in a timely manner. Depending on the test that you took, your lab results may look different. Our specialists are here on hand to help you in understanding your results. Please do not hesitate to contact us if your results are unclear.

In the case that your healthcare professional has additional testing that needs to be performed, we will inform you as soon as possible. NuraHealth respects your time and will work with you on scheduling any follow up appointments. If there are any issues that you experience with scheduling, please do not hesitate to contact us.

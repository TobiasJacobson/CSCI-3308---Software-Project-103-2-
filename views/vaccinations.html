Vaccinations

Vaccinations are very important to living your everyday life and are also required in many school districts. Getting vaccinated not only helps to protect yourself, but also to protect those with suppressed immune systms. There are various vaccines that are recommended for you based on whether you are a child, teenager, or adult. Please rest assured that both the CDC and FDA take many steps to ensure that all vaccines are safe.

When you are due for a vaccination or follow up booster shot, we will inform you as soon as possible. NuraHealth respects your time and will work with you on scheduling any follow up appointments. If there are any issues that you experience with scheduling, please do not hesitate to contact us.
